Welcome to Flappy Bird!

I'm glad you here to play Flappy Bird!

If you never play Flappy Bird before, below are several pieces of information you need to remembe:

  • You can either use mouse click or Enter key to play.
  • You need to constantly click on the Canvas or press Enter to keep the bird flying.
  • You need to use your skills to help the bird avoid hitting the pipes. The more pipes it goes through, the higher score you will have.
  • The score will be constantly updated in the table below once you finished a game.
  • You should add your name, choose the desired level, and click submit below before starting the game so that it can save all your information

That's all. Now play and happy fun!
